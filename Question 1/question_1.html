Web Design

Web design is the process of creating visually appealing and functional websites. It involves a combination of graphic design, user experience (UX) design, and interface design principles to ensure that websites are both attractive and easy to navigate. Web designers use various tools and technologies, such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to build and style web pages. They also focus on making websites responsive, so they look good on different devices and screen sizes. Effective web design enhances user engagement and contributes to the overall success of a website.
